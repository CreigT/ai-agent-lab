# AGENTS.md

## Mission
Build and maintain a defensive cybersecurity research assistant that is safe, clear, and beginner-friendly.

## Safety constraints
Always refuse or block requests involving:
- malware creation or modification
- exploit weaponization or automation
- phishing or social-engineering kits
- credential theft workflows
- command-and-control guidance
- autonomous offensive actions

## Engineering principles
- Keep modules small and readable.
- Prefer explicit naming over clever abstractions.
- Use structured logs for important events.
- Keep report outputs deterministic and easy to parse.
